Summoners War:Starting Out- Tips and Tricks

The game is really massive and takes a while to load. Once loaded you will have to play a walk thru guide that goes through
the main points. It can drag on a bit but is very helpful. I recommend listening and paying attention.
I would try to request as many friends as possible starting out to help get thru the harder stages.
You can use a Rep Monster(Friends) to help once a day for every friend you possess.
Also try not to use any scrolls you acquire at first as they give you some decent monsters to play with as you progress.
This game has a massive following and they have weekly/monthly events that had out all sorts of in game goodies,
so make sure you check the event icon daily. You will be able to fight in the arena as well, so take advantage of that.
You will get crystals from winning the rival stages. They recently added a new progression tool to help guide users.
If you keep up with that you will get scrolls, crystals, and mainy other items to help with you along the way.
