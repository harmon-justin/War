Summoners War:About

Monster:User can summon a variety of monsters to use.
Monsters range in skill level by stars, One Star is lowest-Five Star is highest summon. Monsters can be evolved up to Six Star.
Every Monster has a different set of tools and are classified by Attack, Support, Defense, HP.
Monsters are also divided into Attributes and Each Attribute has an advantage over another Attribute.
Water > Fire > Wind > Water. Also have two other Attributes Light and Dark.

Runes: Like Monsters, Runes are classified by Stars ranging from One Star to Six Star. One Star Runes have the lowest possible attributes. Six Star have the highest.
Runes also are ranked by Normal, Magic, Rare, Hero, Legendary. Again you can upgrade runes but the higher the rune to begin then the better the attribute role will be.

Mana: This is gained by beating levels in ht egame and also can be produced by the hour with building in game.

Energy: You will need this to enter levels.

Crystals: In came currency to buy a variety of stuff from Monster summoning scrolls to more Mana or Energy.
